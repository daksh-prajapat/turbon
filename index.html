About Turban

Committed to Finding

Your Perfect Home

Welcome to Turban Builders & Promoters – formerly recognized as the Century Group, where the timeless values of tradition meet the limitless possibilities of innovation. Guided by the principles of Lord Shri Ram, we are reshaping the real estate sector with a vision that prioritizes not just structures but the lives and aspirations of those who inhabit them.

At Turban Group, we believe every great achievement begins as a dream. As the saying goes, “Everything is created twice, first in the mind and then in reality.” With this in mind, we approach every project as an opportunity to transform bold ideas into architectural masterpieces. Each project in Turban Group is a landmark in its own right, designed to inspire and endure for generations.
